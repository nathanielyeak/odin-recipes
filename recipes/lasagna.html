Lasagna

Description

It's a lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • Boneless country style pork rib
  • italian sausage - remove skin
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • White Wine
  • 3x Cans Ground Peeled Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Pecorino
  • Parmesan
  • Shredded Mozarella - low moisture
  • Ricotta - dab it on

Steps

Sauce

  1. 1. Brown pork 5-7mins - salt
  2. 2. Remove pork
  3. 3. Add sausages broken up
  4. 4. Remove sausage
  5. 5. Add mirepoix
  6. 6. Add garlic
  7. 7. Add dry white wine
  8. 8. Add basil
  9. 9. Add tomatoes
  10. 10. Add salt, black pepper and red pepper flakes
  11. 11. Add meat back
  12. 12. Cook low and slow for 90mins
  13. 13. Remove basil and pork (to shred and add back)
  14. 14. Add fresh shredded basil

Layering

  1. Sauce, Pasta, Sauce, Cheese
  2. 1. Push down - want it to be compact
  3. 2. Pasta can overlap a bit
  4. 3. Go to the corners
  5. 4. Last layer just grated cheese, not ricotta
  6. On baking tray
  7. Foil on loosely
  8. 350 degrees
  9. 30 mins with foil on
  10. 30 mins with foil off
  11. Rest for at least 30 mins
  12. run knife along outside edge
